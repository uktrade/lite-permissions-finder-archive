As you are exporting specific dual-use items to another EU country, you do not need an export licence. Most dual-use items can be moved freely within the EU.

You must follow these rules when moving dual-use items within the EU:

  • Keep documentation, eg sales contracts, order confirmations and invoices, for at least three years from the end of the calendar year in which a transfer of items took place
  • Indicate on all documentation that the items would require a licence if exported from the EU
  • Produce documentation to authorities on request

You can contact the Export Control Organisation, by email at eco.help@trade.gsi.gov.uk or by phone on 020 7215 4594, if you still think you might need a licence.
