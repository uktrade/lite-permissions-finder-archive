You are unlikely to need an import licence

You do not need a licence to import the majority of goods from another EU country into the UK.

You do need to follow special rules to move some types of item within the EU, for example plants or plant products that can host quarantine pests and diseases.

If you think you may need a licence, or for further advice on import controls, you can contact the Import Licensing Branch by email enquiries.ilb@trade.gsi.gov.uk or by phone on 01642 364333.
