You need a manufacturer licence


You need a manufacturer licence that authorises import if you want to import human medicine and other medical products. Read about how to apply for a manufacturer licence.

Licences are administered by the Medicines and Healthcare products Regulatory Agency. Use the MHRA Process Licensing Portal.

Unlicensed medicinal products

If you are importing unlicensed medicinal products, known as 'specials', you may need a specials licence. Read about supplying specials.
