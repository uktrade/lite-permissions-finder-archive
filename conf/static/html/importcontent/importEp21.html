You may not be allowed to import your items

You are not allowed to import goods that could be used for torture. Read about equipment that is banned under torture legislation.

You can import certain goods if they are solely for public display. If you think this may apply to your items, or for further advice on import controls, you can contact the Import Licensing Branch by email enquiries.ilb@bis.gsi.gov.uk or by phone on 01642 364333.
