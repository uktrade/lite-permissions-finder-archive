You may not be allowed to import your items


You are not allowed to import goods into the EU that originated in Crimea or Sevastopol. Read about sanctions on international trade with Crimea

If you still think you may be eligible for a licence, or for further advice on import controls, you can contact the Import Licensing Branch by email enquiries.ilb@bis.gsi.gov.uk or by phone on 01642 364333.
