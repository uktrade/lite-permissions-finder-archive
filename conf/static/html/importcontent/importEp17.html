Your goods must be classified before they can be imported


Classification identifies the hazards posed by non-military explosive substances.

For non-military explosives, you can apply to the Health and Safety Executive (HSE) for classification. Read about how to apply for classification.

For military explosives, you will need classification from the Explosives Storage and Transport Committee of the Ministry of Defence, but you should first contact the HSE by email cad.explosives@hse.gsi.gov.uk to discuss your import.

Read more about the classification of explosives.
