You have not found your item in the UK Military List. This does not mean that your item is not controlled.

Warning: Always double-check if your items are controlled. If you export controlled items without the correct export licence you could:
  • have your items seized
  • face a fine
  • serve up to 10 years in prison

What you should do next

  1. Get someone who knows the technical details of your details of your item to help you check the choices you've made against the technical specifications of your item.
  2. Go back to verify the type of item and which category it falls under within the UK Military List.
  3. Consider whether your item is specially designed for military use or not. If your item is not military than you should consider the dual use list.

Export control licenses

You cannot export items without a licence if:

  • you know or suspect they'll be used in a weapons of mass destruction programme or delivery system (also known as 'end-use controls')
  • you're exporting dual-use items that do not usually need an export licence but you know or suspect they might be incorporated into military equipment for use in embargoed countries
  • there are embargoes, sanctions or other restrictions which cover your export destination
