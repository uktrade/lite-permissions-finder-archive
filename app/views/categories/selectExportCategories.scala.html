@detailPanel("mil"){Military equipment and firearms} {

This covers all military items, such as weapons, weapon-delivery systems, explosives, munitions, ammunition and ordnance, and military vehicles. It also includes software and source code to enable the use of military goods, and technology such as manuals, designs, models or blueprints for goods that themselves require an export licence.

} @detailPanel("dual-use"){Dual-use goods, software and technology}{

Dual-use items are civilian goods, equipment, technology, documents or diagrams that can also be used for military purposes. They can be raw materials, components or finished systems.

Dual-use items are typically high-tech, advanced products, often with military standard specifications.

You should choose this category if you are exporting goods that you think could be used for military purposes, even if you are not sure.

} @detailPanel("torture-restraint"){Goods that could be used for torture, restraint or execution}{

This covers items that could be used for capital punishment, torture or other cruel, inhuman or degrading treatment or punishment. It also includes goods designed for the execution of human beings such as gallows, electric chairs and automatic drug-injection systems.

} @detailPanel("radioactive"){Radioactive goods}{

This covers high-activity radioactive sources as defined by the International Atomic Energy Agency.

Read guidance on controls on radioactive sources for more information on the items covered in this category.

} @detailPanel("chemicals-cosmetics"){Cosmetics, chemicals, and pesticides}{

This includes toiletries and beauty products, detergents, disinfectants, domestic cleaners, industrial chemicals, chemical raw materials and food-handling materials.

} @detailPanel("arts-cultural"){Arts and cultural goods}{

This includes cultural artefacts, works of art, antiques (including weapons), manuscripts and archaeological items.

} @detailPanel("plants-animals"){Plants and animals}{

This includes live animals and birds, non-edible flowers and plants, seeds and other wildlife.

} @detailPanel("Food"){Food}{

This includes any products suitable for human consumption, including fresh food and processed food.

} @detailPanel("medicines-drugs"){Medicines and drugs}{

This includes medicinal products, prescription drugs and controlled drugs.

} @detailPanel("technical-assistance"){Technical assistance}{

This covers any technical support for military or dual-use items. This includes:

  • repairs
  • development
  • manufacture
  • testing
  • maintenance
  • any other technical service

You should choose this category if you are providing training, instruction or advice, working knowledge, skills or consulting services, either in person, verbally or electronically.

} @detailPanel("financial-assistance"){Financing and financial assistance}{
