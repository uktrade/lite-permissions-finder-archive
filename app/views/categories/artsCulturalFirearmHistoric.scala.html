Cultural goods exports are licensed by the Arts Council. As your export is a gun or firearm, you may also need an export licence from the Export Control Organisation.

Arts Council licence

You may need a licence for goods over 50 years old, depending on their value.

You need a licence to export cultural goods on either a temporary or permanent basis.

Read Arts Council guidance on export licensing for cultural goods (PDF, 520KB) (opens in a new tab).

The Arts Council also issue an open general export licence (OGEL) for cultural goods. Read the licence conditions (PDF, 87KB) (opens in a new tab)
